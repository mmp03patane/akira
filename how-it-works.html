THE PROCESS

From first call to live website

No jargon. No surprises. Just a clear, simple process designed around busy NDIS providers who have better things to do than manage a web project.

GETTING STARTED

Free Discovery Call

We start with a relaxed 30-minute call. No sales pressure — just an honest conversation about your practice, your goals, and what you need your website to do. We'll ask you about your services, your participants, your geographic area, and your registration status.

By the end of the call, we'll know exactly what to build and you'll know exactly what to expect.

📞

30 minutes via phone or Zoom. You don't need to prepare anything.

CONTENT

Conversion Blueprint Interview

Most providers dread writing their own website copy. You don't have to. We record our discovery call and use it to write conversion-focused copy that sounds like you — not like a corporate brochure.

We'll also send you a simple checklist of what we need: your logo (if you have one), any photos, your ABN, registration details, and a few key details about your services. That's it.

📋

Simple checklist sent via email. Most clients complete it in under 20 minutes.

COMPLIANCE

Design & Build

Once we have your content, we get to work. Your site is built on WordPress — the world's most widely used CMS — so it's easy for you to update yourself later, and easy to hand off to any developer if you ever need to.

Every Akira site is built to WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility standards, includes your required NDIS policy pages, a compliant complaints pathway, and is structured to satisfy the NDIS Quality and Safeguards Commission.

🛠️

Built on WordPress. You own the code, the content, and the domain — always.

DELIVERY

Your Review — Two Rounds Included

We'll send you a private preview link so you can review the site before anything goes live. You get two rounds of revisions included — that's enough to get everything exactly right without the project dragging on forever.

Most clients use their first round for content tweaks and their second for final polish. We'll walk you through the preview on a short call if that's easier than written feedback.

✏️

2 revision rounds included. Additional rounds available if needed.

LAUNCH

Hosting Setup & Launch

You choose your own hosting provider — we'll recommend the right plan for your budget and needs, help you set it up, and handle the full deployment. Popular options include SiteGround, Kinsta, and WP Engine. Most plans start from around $10–$20/month.

Once hosting is sorted, we deploy your site, configure your SSL certificate, connect your domain, set up Google Analytics, and run a full pre-launch checklist before anything goes public.

🚀

You own your hosting. No lock-in to Akira infrastructure — ever.

POST LAUNCH

Handover & Ongoing Support

At handover we walk you through your WordPress dashboard so you can make basic updates yourself — change a photo, update your hours, add a new service. You don't need to call us for every small change.

If you'd prefer someone to handle updates, security, and ongoing improvements for you, we offer a monthly support plan. It's optional — but most providers find it worthwhile.

🤝

Full WordPress training included at handover. Ongoing support available monthly.
