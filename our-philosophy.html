OUR PHILOSOPHY

Built on principle, not just code

We didn't start Akira to build websites. We started it because NDIS providers deserve a digital partner who actually understands their world — and takes it seriously.

"Bright, clear, intelligent."

In Japanese, 明 means bright, clear, and intelligent — three things we believe every NDIS provider's digital presence should be. Bright enough to stand out. Clear enough that participants and families immediately understand who you are and what you offer. Intelligent enough to work as hard as you do.

These aren't just words on a page. They're the standard we hold every website, every integration, and every client relationship to.

01

Transparency

No surprises. Ever.

We know what it takes to make the leap from employee or sole trader to registered NDIS provider. It's one of the most stressful transitions a health professional can make. You're navigating registration requirements, practice standards, audit frameworks, insurance, and a hundred other things — all while trying to actually deliver great services.

The last thing you need is a web designer who gives you a vague quote, moves the goalposts, and sends you an invoice you weren't expecting.

At Akira, our pricing is published. Our process is documented. Our timelines are guaranteed. When you come to us, you know exactly what you're getting, what it costs, and when it will be done. That clarity is intentional — because it's what we'd want if the roles were reversed.

"Our pricing is on the website. Our process is on the website. We have nothing to hide and everything to prove."
02

Support

A partner, not a vendor.

Most web designers build your site, hand you the keys, and disappear. You're left with a WordPress login, no training, and no idea who to call when something breaks or your business grows in a direction you didn't anticipate.

We don't operate that way. When you work with Akira, you're not just buying a website — you're gaining a long-term digital partner who understands the NDIS sector, understands small business, and is invested in your success beyond launch day.

That means business advice when you're scaling. Marketing guidance when you're trying to reach new participants. A direct line to someone who knows your site inside out when you need a change made. And honest counsel when you're making decisions that affect your digital presence.

We grow when you grow. That's the only business model that makes sense to us.

"You are not investing in a website. You are investing in a business relationship built around your growth."
03

Connectivity

Everything has a purpose.

A website that sits in isolation is just an expensive brochure. It doesn't talk to your booking system. It doesn't feed leads into your CRM. It doesn't trigger a follow-up when someone fills out a contact form. It just sits there looking pretty while you manually copy information from one system to another.

We build websites that are the centre of your digital ecosystem — not separate from it. That means your Cliniko or Splose appointments connect to your site. Your Xero invoices trigger your review automation. Your contact forms feed directly into your client management system. Your Google Business Profile, your email provider, your SMS platform — all of it talks to each other.

The result is a practice that runs more smoothly, wastes less of your time on admin, and delivers a more professional experience to every participant and family who interacts with you.

"A disconnected website is a liability. A connected one is an asset that compounds over time."
04

Integrity

We tell you what you need to hear.

The NDIS sector attracts providers who genuinely care about the people they support. It also attracts opportunists who see a large government scheme and want a slice of it without the substance to back it up. The Commission exists precisely to weed out the latter.

We take compliance seriously — not as a box-ticking exercise, but because the participants your practice serves deserve providers who operate with genuine integrity. That means we won't build you a website that makes misleading claims. We won't help you imply NDIS endorsement you don't have. We won't cut corners on accessibility or privacy compliance just because a client doesn't know to ask.

If something you ask for isn't in your best interest — or your participants' best interest — we'll tell you. Even if it costs us a sale.

"We work exclusively in the NDIS space. Our reputation lives or dies on the quality of what we build and the honesty of how we operate."
05

Clarity

We speak business, not jargon.

Most web developers will talk to you about APIs, CSS frameworks, server configurations, and plugin ecosystems. That's not a conversation — it's a power move designed to make you feel dependent on them.

We have a background in Law and Business. We understand that what you care about isn't how your website is built — it's what it does for your practice. How many enquiries did it generate this month? Did it make the registration process easier? Are families finding you on Google? Is the booking system reducing admin time?

Every conversation we have is framed around outcomes you can measure, not technical complexity you can't. You'll always know what we're doing, why we're doing it, and what result it's designed to achieve.

"You speak therapy. We speak business and tech. Our job is to translate."
