PLAIN ENGLISH

What the guarantee actually means.

Most guarantees are full of fine print designed to protect the person making the promise, not the person receiving it. This one is different. Here's exactly how it works — no ambiguity, no escape hatches.

When The Clock Starts

The day we have everything.

The 7-day clock doesn't start when you sign the contract or pay the deposit. It starts the day we confirm in writing that we have received absolutely everything we need from you — your photos, your logo, your colour preferences, any inspiration sites, and your copy if you're providing it.

Until we have the full picture, the timer hasn't started. This protects both of us — you don't get penalised for our slow start, and we don't get penalised for waiting on assets that haven't arrived yet.

📧

You'll receive a written confirmation email from us the day the clock officially starts. Keep it — it's your proof.

What We Need From You

Come prepared. The checklist is non-negotiable.

To trigger the guarantee, you need to provide everything on the following checklist in one go. Partial submissions don't start the clock — because building a website with half the assets leads to a half-built website.

✓
Logo files

PNG or SVG, ideally both light and dark versions. No logo yet? Let us know upfront and we'll use typography.

✓
Photos

Of you, your practice, your team — whatever you have. Professional photos are ideal but not required. High resolution phone photos work fine.

✓
Colour preferences

If you have brand colours, provide the hex codes. If not, tell us what direction you're thinking — warm, cool, bold, minimal — and we'll handle it.

✓
Inspiration sites

Two or three websites you like the look or feel of. They don't need to be in the NDIS space — just show us the aesthetic direction.

✓
ABN and registration details

Your registered business name, ABN, NDIS registration number and groups. Required for compliance display on your site.

✓
Services and service area

A plain list of what you offer and where. No need to write it up professionally — that's our job after our discovery call.

✓
Domain name

Your existing domain or your chosen domain if you haven't registered one yet. Need help choosing? We'll advise before the clock starts.

What Gets Delivered

Version 1. Not the finished article.

The 7-day guarantee covers delivery of your Version 1 — a complete, launch-ready website built to spec, reflecting everything discussed in our discovery call and everything provided in your asset checklist.

Version 1 is not a rough draft. It's a fully built, fully functional website that you could publish today if you wanted to. But it's not the final version after revisions — it's the starting point for your two included rounds of feedback.

💡

If we deliver Version 1 on Day 4 and you request revisions, the guarantee is still met. The 7 days covers our build time — not the revision process.

When The Clock Stops

Day 7. Close of business. That's the deadline.

The clock stops at 5:00pm AEST on the 7th business day after the start confirmation email. Business days are Monday to Friday — weekends and Victorian public holidays don't count.

If we send you the Version 1 preview link before 5:00pm on Day 7, the guarantee is met. If we miss that deadline for any reason within our control — we forfeit our fee. Simple as that.

📅

Business days only. Weekends and Victorian public holidays are excluded from the 7-day count.

If We Miss The Deadline

You don't pay. No arguments.

If we fail to deliver Version 1 by 5:00pm AEST on Day 7 — for any reason within our control — your invoice is voided. You owe nothing. Any deposit paid is refunded in full within 2 business days.

We've built this guarantee into our process because we're confident we can meet it every time. But putting it in writing means you can hold us to it — and that's exactly the point.

🔒

This guarantee is included in your service agreement, signed before any work begins.

What can void the guarantee.

There are only a small number of circumstances where the guarantee doesn't apply. All of them are within your control, not ours.

✗
Incomplete asset delivery

If the checklist isn't complete when you submit it, the clock hasn't started. Partial submissions don't trigger the guarantee.

✗
Scope changes after the clock starts

Requesting additional pages, new features, or significant changes to the brief after the build has begun pauses the clock until the scope is agreed.

✗
Delayed responses on critical questions

If we need a quick clarification during the build and don't hear back for more than 24 hours on a business day, the clock pauses until we do.

✗
Third party delays outside our control

Domain registration issues, hosting platform outages, or delays caused by your existing platform are not within our control and don't trigger the guarantee penalty.
